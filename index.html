What?

David DeSandro's jQuery Masonry loops through the items and places each as close to the top as possible.

JQuery Masonry Ordered tries to preserve the previous/next relationship of the elements in the sequence.
Download the jquery.​masonry.​ordered.js script

Is there a tradeoff?

Yes, Masonry Ordered usually takes up visibly more vertical space.
